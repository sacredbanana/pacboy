Info

This is a Pacman inspired game developed by myself for CITS3402 Agile Web Development at the University of Western Australia.


Your aim is to collect all the pellets and not be touched by an enemy. You can disable enemies by touching them when they are in a vulnerable state (if you collect a super pellet) or by shooting them with a weapon.


The controls are:

  • UP
  • DOWN
  • LEFT
  • RIGHT
  • SPACE (SHOOT) **Not working yet**
